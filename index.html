About the Show

Aaj Ka Dhamaka is UNC’s premier collegiate South Asian dance competition. Expect big energy, bold visuals, and a crowd that’s ready from tip-off to the final buzzer. Proceeds support Kiran, Inc.

Quick Facts
  • • Collegiate teams from across the country
  • • Professional judging panel
  • • Space-Jam inspired production
  • • In partnership with NCAAT (Fall Cohort)

History

2020
AKD relaunches at UNC with a sold-out showcase.
2022
Expanded to a regional invitational; first visiting judges panel.
2024
Partnerships deepen with community orgs; increased philanthropy.
