Partners & Community Support

NCAAT

We’re proud to partner with North Carolina Asian Americans Together (NCAAT), a nonpartisan organization building Asian American and Pacific Islander power in North Carolina through civic engagement, leadership development, and community education. Aaj Ka Dhamaka is part of NCAAT’s Fall Cohort, and we’re excited to uplift their mission of participation and representation across our community.

Learn more about NCAAT

KIRAN

A portion of proceeds from Aaj Ka Dhamaka will be donated to Kiran, Inc., a North Carolina nonprofit serving South Asian survivors of domestic violence and sexual assault. Kiran provides culturally specific, confidential services, including crisis response, safety planning, counseling, referrals, and language-accessible assistance. Your support helps Kiran sustain life-saving resources for our community.
