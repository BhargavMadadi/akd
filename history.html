Our Journey

From a small campus idea to a Space-Jam level showdown.

2019

AKD is Born

UNC students come together to launch Aaj Ka Dhamaka – a South Asian dance showcase with big energy and even bigger dreams.

2021

First Major Expansion

Teams from across the East Coast join the lineup, turning AKD into a full weekend of dance, music, and community.

2023

Legends Partnership

AKD becomes an official Legends partner competition, raising the stakes and the production value.

2025

Space Jam Era

A Space-Jam themed experience takes over UNC – complete with intergalactic visuals, charity partnerships, and a sold-out crowd.

2026 & Beyond

What’s Next?

Bigger stages, bolder stories, and more chances for teams to leave it all on the court – and the stage.
