Our Journey

From a late-night idea to a Space-Jam-level South Asian dance showdown at UNC.

2019

AKD Is Born

UNC students launch Aaj Ka Dhamaka as a campus-wide South Asian dance showcase — small budget, big energy, and a packed crowd.

2021

Leveling Up

Teams from across the East Coast join the lineup. AKD evolves from a showcase into a full intercollegiate competition weekend with mixers, workshops, and after-events.

2023

Production Glow-Up

Bigger stage, tighter lighting, and cinematic visuals. AKD starts leaning into themed storylines, turning the show into a full experience from doors-open to final awards.

2024

Legends Era

AKD joins the Legends circuit as an official partner competition, bringing nationally ranked teams, higher stakes, and a new level of polish to Chapel Hill.

2025

Space Jam AKD

The Space-Jam theme lands: galaxies, planets, and a full-court mashup of basketball hype and South Asian dance. Glow effects, custom visuals, and a sold-out Memorial Hall crowd.

2026 & Beyond

What’s Next?

More teams, louder crowds, and even wilder themes. AKD continues to grow as a staple of UNC’s South Asian community — a place where teams leave it all on the court, and on the stage.
